ABOUT ME

Who am I?

I am a multimedia storyteller, influential speaker, and journalist from Seattle. I am University of Washington alumni, amd I earned my B.A. in political science and journalism & public interest communication; coupled with a diversity studies minor. I am a third generation media professional. Some of my enjoyments involve anywhere with an ocean view, delicious food, family, and live music.

What do I do?

I am a multimedia storyteller. I specialize in film production (from pre- to post-production), writing, photography, public speaking, and more.

What do you WANT to do?

My vision is to use storytelling as a means to empower individuals to live authentic, purpose-driven that inform, equip, and engage collectives within spheres of influence. These spheres includes, but aren't limited to: family, religion, education, media, entertainment, business, and government. I want to serve by accurately showcasing the stories of creatives, professionals and communities through a multimedia approach.
